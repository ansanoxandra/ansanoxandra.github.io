### **About Me** ♥

Hello! My name is ♥ **XANDRA NICOLE ANSANO** ♥, a second-year college student from Davao del Norte, currently studying Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) at Davao del Norte State College.

Design has always been more than a skill for me — it's how I see the world. Growing up around ink, paper, and printing machines at our family business **ART-TECH Computer Services** shaped my understanding of how colors work and how visuals communicate, long before I ever formally studied it.

Now, as a BSIS student, I'm merging that real-world experience with a deeper passion for graphic design and UI/UX design — combining creativity with technology to build experiences that are both beautiful and functional. As part of my program, I am developing skills in systems analysis and design, which aligns closely with my goal of becoming a UI/UX designer in the future.

My goal is to continue growing as a designer and IT professional, creating work that makes a real impact. I don't just make things look good — I make them mean something.
