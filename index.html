About Me

Hi, I'm Xandra Nicole Ansano, a 2nd year BSIS student at Davao del Norte State College.


I grew up around our family's printing business, ART-TECH Computer Services, so I've been around design, colors, and layouts for a long time. That's probably why I got into graphic design — it just felt natural.


I took Arts and Design in senior high school, and since then I've been into poster-making, photo editing, and painting. Now that I'm in college, I'm also starting to learn about coding and IT-related stuff.


I offer design services on the side — mostly tarpaulins, ID photo editing, and document layouts. It's something I enjoy doing and I'm always open to new projects.
