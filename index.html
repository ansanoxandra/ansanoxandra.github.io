About Me

Hello. My name is XANDRA NICOLE ANSANO, a second-year college student currently taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Systems at Davao del Norte State College.


Design has always been more than a skill for me — it is how I see the world. Growing up around ink, paper, and printing machines at our family business ART-TECH Computer Services shaped my understanding of how colors work and how visuals communicate, long before I ever formally studied it.


Now, as a BSIS student, I am merging that real-world experience with a deeper passion for graphic design and UI/UX design — combining creativity with technology to build experiences that are both beautiful and functional.


I am currently improving my knowledge in Adobe Photoshop, digital layout design, and coding. As part of my BSIS program, I am also developing skills in systems analysis and design, which aligns closely with my goal of becoming a UI/UX designer in the future.


My goal is to continue growing as a designer and IT professional, creating work that makes a real impact. I do not just make things look good — I make them mean something.
